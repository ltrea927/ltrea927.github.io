About Me

Living the Dream as a Photographer

My name is Lauren and I would like to tell you a little bit about my story and all the adventures and travel locations I've gone to. I'm currently a photographer living the dream as a full-time travel photo journalist. I've had the honor and privilege to meet and work with many people around the world.  

Most of the countries I've had the privilege of visiting were Italy, Spain, Norway, Switzerland, China, Japan, Africa (Burundi, 
Somalia, Mozambique, Somalia, and Niger), Brazil, Mexico, Yemen, Jamaica, New Zealand, Honduras, Puerto Rico, and all 50 states of the U.S.   

Each country I've gone to has a different story and a different way of life. They have all had a tremendous impact on me because we take what we have in our day to day lives here in the U.S when those in other countries don't have much of anything.
